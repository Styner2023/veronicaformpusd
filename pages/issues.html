Priority Issues

Building a brighter future for Monterey Peninsula Schools

Our Focus Areas

01. Improving Access to Quality Education

As a former educator in local schools, I've seen firsthand the impact of limited resources on our students. My vision is to ensure every child has the tools they need to succeed.

  • Reduce class sizes from current average of 28 to 22 students
  • Expand after-school programs to reach 50% more students
  • Invest $2 million in modern learning technologies
  • Provide 40 hours of annual professional development for teachers

Past Achievement

Successfully implemented a pilot program that reduced class sizes by 15% in three local schools, resulting in a 20% improvement in standardized test scores.

02. Promoting Cultural Diversity and Inclusion

My Chicana Indigenous heritage has taught me the value of diversity. I'm committed to creating an inclusive environment that celebrates all cultures.

  • Implement culturally responsive teaching practices in all schools
  • Increase diversity in curriculum by 30%
  • Host monthly multicultural events in each school
  • Provide bilingual support for 100% of ELL students and families

Past Achievement

Developed a district-wide cultural awareness program that increased student engagement by 25% among minority students.

03. Enhancing STEM Programs

In our rapidly evolving world, STEM education is crucial. I aim to prepare our students for the jobs of tomorrow.

  • Increase STEM course offerings by 40%
  • Partner with 10 local tech companies for mentorship programs
  • Upgrade science labs in all middle and high schools
  • Establish coding clubs in 100% of our schools

Past Achievement

Launched a successful robotics program that led to a 30% increase in students pursuing STEM majors in college.

04. Strengthening Community-School Partnerships

I believe in the power of community. By working together, we can provide unparalleled support for our students.

  • Increase parent involvement by 50% through regular workshops
  • Establish internship programs with 20 local businesses
  • Partner with 5 community organizations for after-school programs
  • Create a 15-member community advisory board

Past Achievement

Established a successful parent-teacher collaboration program that improved student attendance by 15%.

"Veronica's dedication to our schools is unparalleled. Her innovative ideas and commitment to diversity have already made a significant impact in our community."

- Maria Rodriguez, Monterey Peninsula Parent

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world. Together, we can create a school system that empowers every student to reach their full potential."
