Overview

I am honored to run for the Monterey Peninsula Schools Board and I am committed to making a positive difference in the lives of our students and families.

On this website, you will find information about my background, my vision for the future, and the key priorities that guide my work.

Priority Issues

01. Improving access to quality education

Ensuring every student has access to high-quality educational resources and opportunities.

02. Promoting cultural diversity and inclusion

Celebrating our community's diverse heritage and fostering an inclusive learning environment.

03. Enhancing STEM programs

Strengthening science, technology, engineering, and mathematics education to prepare students for future careers.

04. Strengthening community-school partnerships

Building strong relationships between schools, families, and local organizations to support student success.

Join me in creating better schools for all!
